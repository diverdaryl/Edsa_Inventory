Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: February 9, 2026

At OOHPhilippines.com, your privacy matters. This page explains what info we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

By using this website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic info when you:

  • Fill out a contact form
  • Send us an email
  • Sign up for updates or newsletters
  • Submit a listing or inquiry

This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Phone number
  • Any message you choose to share

We also collect non-personal info such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and time and date of visits. This helps us improve the site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your info to:

  • Reply to your messages or inquiries
  • Connect you with the right OOH partners
  • Improve our website and content
  • Share updates, if you choose to receive them
  • Keep the site safe and running smooth

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Cookies and Advertising

OOHPhilippines.com may use cookies to understand how visitors use the site, improve user experience, and track basic site traffic. You can turn off cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic. This site may also display ads served by Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. How We Protect Your Data

We take reasonable steps to protect your data using secure systems, limited access to stored info, and standard safety practices. No system is 100% secure, but we work hard to keep your info safe.

5. Sharing of Information

We only share your info when:

  • You ask us to connect you with a media owner, supplier, or partner
  • It is needed to provide a service you requested
  • The law requires it

We do not share your data with random third parties.

6. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to other websites. We are not responsible for their privacy rules or content. Please check their privacy policies too.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Ask what data we have about you
  • Request corrections
  • Ask us to delete your info
  • Opt out of emails anytime

Just contact us and we will act within a reasonable time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new date.
