Billboard Advertising in Cebu

Reach the Philippines’ second-largest urban market across Cebu City, Mandaue, and Mactan

Cebu is the Philippines’ second-largest urban market — a booming metro of over 3 million people with its own premium billboard corridors across Cebu City, Mandaue, and Lapu-Lapu.

Why Advertise in Metro Cebu?

Metro Cebu combines a dense urban core with the country's busiest domestic tourism gateway. Its economy spans IT-BPM, shipping, manufacturing, and tourism, and its consumers are reached far more affordably than Metro Manila's. For brands expanding beyond Luzon, Cebu is almost always the first stop — and OOH is one of the strongest channels in a market where radio and out-of-home habits remain strong.

Key Billboard Locations in Metro Cebu

Osmeña Boulevard & Fuente Osmeña Circle

The historic heart of Cebu City's CBD, with dense pedestrian and vehicle traffic around the rotunda, hospitals, and universities.

Cebu IT Park & Lahug

The BGC of Cebu — BPO workers, nightlife, and a young professional audience with strong spending power.

Cebu Business Park / Ayala Center

Premium retail catchment surrounded by corporate towers and hotels.

Mandaue City & A.C. Cortes / UN Avenue

The industrial and logistics spine connecting Cebu City to Mactan; heavy truck and commuter volume.

Marcelo Fernan & Mactan–Mandaue Bridges

Chokepoint traffic between the mainland and Mactan Island — high dwell time during peak hours.

Lapu-Lapu & Airport Road

Tourism-driven exposure: airport arrivals, resort transfers, and the Mactan economic zone workforce.

Typical Cebu Billboard Rates

Cebu rates sit between provincial and Metro Manila levels. Static faces commonly range from ₱40,000 to ₱150,000 per month, with premium CBD and bridge-approach sites higher, and LED spots available on rotation in the CBD. See our nationwide cost guide and confirm current rates with operators.

Planning Tips for Cebu

  1. Split mainland vs Mactan. Tourists concentrate on Mactan; residents and office workers on the mainland. Choose corridors accordingly.
  2. Mind Sinulog season. January's Sinulog festival multiplies foot traffic — book Q4–Q1 placements early.
  3. Pair with provincial Visayas. Advertisers often bundle Cebu with Bacolod and Iloilo — see our provincial billboard guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do billboards cost in Cebu?

Static billboards in Metro Cebu commonly range from ₱40,000 to ₱150,000 per month, with premium CBD locations and bridge approaches priced higher. LED spots are available at lower entry costs on rotation.

Where are the best billboard locations in Cebu?

Top corridors include Osmeña Boulevard and Fuente Circle, Cebu IT Park and Lahug, Cebu Business Park, Mandaue's UN Avenue, the Mactan bridge approaches, and the airport road in Lapu-Lapu.
